Mac n Cheese

Easy to wind up in 15 minutes homemade mac 'n cheese

Ingredients

  • Maceroni pasta
  • CHeddar cheese
  • Milk
  • flour

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles: Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente.
    Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.

  2. Make the cheese sauce: Melt butter, then whisk in the flour.
    Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses.
    Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick.
    Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.

  3. Make the topping: Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown.
    Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.

  4. Bake the mac and cheese: Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
